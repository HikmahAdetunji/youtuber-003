Got Questions?

The stuff creators always ask before we work together.

How long does a script take? +
Standard turnaround is 24-72 hours from brief approval. For rush projects, an additional fee applies. I never sacrifice quality for speed, if it needs more time, I'll tell you upfront.
Do you work with any niche? +
I specialize in finance, automotive, tech, education, beauty, true crime, health, and documentary content. Other niches? Reach out. If I'm not the best fit, I'll tell you honestly rather than waste your time and budget.
What do you need from me to start? +
A topic, your target audience, video length, and any previous videos I can reference for tone. The more context, the better the script.
How many revisions are included? +
All packages include 2 rounds of revisions. My clients rarely need both; the brief process is thorough enough that first drafts typically land well. Additional revisions are available at a flat rate.
Can you match my channel's existing voice? +
Absolutely, and I'm good at it. Send me 3–5 of your best-performing videos. I'll analyze your delivery style, vocabulary, pacing preferences, and tone before writing a single word.
Do you offer retainer packages? +
Yes. Monthly retainers (4, 8, or 12 scripts/month) come with priority delivery, discounted rates, and a dedicated content strategy session each month. DM me for details.
